Annotated Bibliography

Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of article*. [Source Title](url), *Volume*(Issue), page range.

This paragraph summarizes the source's content, purpose, and relevance to the research topic. It is indented a half-inch from the left margin, is double-spaced, and follows the citation directly without an extra blank line.

Example, B. B. (Year). *Book title*. [Publisher Name](url).

This second entry demonstrates the alphabetical ordering of sources and the consistent formatting required. Each full citation comes first, followed by its corresponding annotation paragraph(s).
